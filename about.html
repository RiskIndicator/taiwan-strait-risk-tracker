About the Index

The Taiwan Strait Risk Tracker is an automated open-source intelligence (OSINT) project designed to quantify geopolitical tension in the Indo-Pacific region.

Methodology

Our algorithm aggregates data from three primary vectors to calculate a daily "Risk Score" (0-100):

  • Economic Anxiety: Real-time volatility comparison between the Taiwan Stock Exchange (specifically semiconductor assets) and the US S&P 500.
  • Military Signaling: Keyword frequency analysis of global reporting on ADIZ (Air Defence Identification Zone) incursions.
  • Rhetorical Sentiment: Natural Language Processing (NLP) analysis of headlines involving cross-strait relations.

Disclaimer

This tool is for informational purposes only. It is not financial advice, nor is it a military predictive system. The score is a reflection of current data availability.
